Translational Medicine Event The Biomedical Engineering Society (BMES), in conjunction with The Clinical and Translational Research Institute (CTRI), is currently planning to host the first annual Translational Medicine Event to take place on May 28th, 2015 in the Telemedicine Building. The goal of the event is to provide undergraduate students with the opportunity to learn about Translational Medicine, and perhaps pursue such research initiatives in graduate school, medical school, and industry. They are currently looking to collaborate with other student organizations and if any TBP members or initiates are interested in becoming involved with this event, particularly if any of you have connections with academia or industry related to translational research please email Neil Jethani for more information or you can attend their weekly meetings every Tuesday from 1-2 pm in the Bioengineering Hall (PFBH) basement.
